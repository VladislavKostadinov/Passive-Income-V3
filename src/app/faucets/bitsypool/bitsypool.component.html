BitsyPool

"Mining Bitcoin is easy peasy on BitsyPool"

Company's statement

"Utilize your GPU and withdraw earnings to your FaucetPay account, it's that simple. Claim extra rewards while mining to increase your earnings."

My point of view

"I've come across this online Bitcoin Cloud Miner. It looks simple and straightforward, doesn't require any investment and doesn't overload your pc or lap-top. The mining process is simple, the platform pays and it also has a faucet, which directly sends a small ammount of Satoshis to your FaucetPay BTC address. Payment is instant and eventhough it doesn't pay much it can be a nice addition to your free-time side-hustles, if you decide to give it a try. I like the straightforward and simple design of the platform."

Tips

  • Register with your FaucetPay BTC (Deposit) Address*
  • Activate the JavaScript and GPU Miner (or download miner app)**
  • Use Faucet***
  • Invite friends and aqcuire referrals

*Registering is pretty straightforward. You provide your FaucetPay BTC (Deposit) Address and you're in. You will need to have a FaucetPay account if you're to use the Bitsypool platform. There is a link for FaucetPay on my Homepage.

**You can immidiately activate the JavaScript (Browser Miner) and the Embedded WebMiner and start earning some Satoshis. You need to earn at least 2 SATS to be able to withdraw and it won't take too long. You can also download the GPU desktop miner(which is faster) and start it (it starts in the console). There is a guide on the website how to activate the dekstop miner and it starts working quietly, generating some SATS. Eventhough it does work (the desktop app), I am not sure if I am going that far. And eventhough it doesn't overheat my lap-top it still puts some burden, if I am currently using a lot of apps and programs, it does seem to generate some extra heat.

***The Faucet can be used every 10 mins, if you've mined at least 10 000 hashes. It provides 1-2 SATS directly into your FaucetPay account.
