Camel BTC

"Camel BTC is a simple PLAY TO EARN game giving real crypto rewards while having fun developing your sandy island. "

Company's statement

"The higher level you reach, the higher income you will receive."

My point of view

"Camel BTC is an addictive mini-browser-game, in which you build virtual structures, collect resources, trade them in the market for Cash Points, go on explorations and have a farm with animals, which also produce "Cash Points" (this is your main income). You can also attack other players for resources. You can exchange "Cash Points" for real money in the form of BTC or ZER. One of the downsides is the low earning rate. But the platform has another interesting aspect. It's advertisement potential. You can advertise your links for a very low price (and even Cash Points). You can also see some very interesting new platforms, that might prove to be to your liking. Also the game is not hard and I use it as a quick "news letter" for new projects in the dynamic "crypto earning" space. And also earn some extra cents along the way, while having fun."

Tips

  • Register with e-mail and password
  • Play the game*
  • Earn Cash Points / Withdraw**
  • Check new links / Advertise***
  • Invite friends and acquire referrals

The icons, I've used for the Tips section are Camel BTC's original images. I did not create or own them.

*The game is quite easy. You structures, which are unlocked as you level up and you resources from them, which you trade for some Cash Points. These points are best used for withdraling crypto or advertising on the platform. An important building is the Barn in which you have animals, that you need to feed with Food (another resource) and from which you receive more Cash Points (this is what I call these points). You can also go on Explorations (on a higher level), trade, get a dailyMystery Box and attack other players. That's it. They reward after completion.

**When you've earned enoughCash Points, you can withdraw them as BTC or ZER to your direct wallet. This will take a while. The Barn and having a lot of animals and food can help you a lot.

***I use the platform mainly to check new links and advertise, while collecting a few cents and having fun. It provides a really nice option for advertising your link for a hefty price. You just need to buy Banner Credits. You can buy 5k credits for 10k Cash Points. Each credit provides 1 Views of your Banner. It can be achieved easily. Also, you can see very up-to-date ads and get notified, when a promising project is gaining popularity.

My earnings

Currently, I haven't earned a lot. Maybe a dollar or so, but I do find the platform fun and use it to advertise my links for a low price and check out new upcomming or established projects in the web space.
Have fun!
