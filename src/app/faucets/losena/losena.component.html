Some of the icons, I've used for the Tips section are Losena's original images. I did not create or own any of them.

*Registration is easy and straight-forward. After completing it and logging into your account, you will be able to explore the website. Note that it is in Russian, so you might find difficulties if you are not familiar with the language, but if you are using Chrome, you can click on translator icon, situated on the right side of the address bar and use Chrome's automatic translator to translate the whole website for your convenience. If you are using a different browser and it does not offer build-in translations by default, you can search for an Add-on or additional information online and you should be able to overcome the language barrier with not that much effort.

**Completing your profile and filling-in the information is very important, because, in doing so, you will receive 5 rating points, and I believe you need at least 7 points to fill-in your mobile number, which unlocks withdrawals. The withdrawals are made to Payeer, WebMoney, Perfect Money, Qiwi and even FaucetPay (as BTC). You can receive 1 rating daily, by clicking on the star icon near your avatar.

***The tasks are easy and well-organized. You have short PTC views with no active window (most of the time), a lot of short youtube videos (again with no active window) and they provide decent rewards in comparison to many other similar platforms. There are some other tasks, such as Shortlinks, Offers, readings letters and more, if you are enthusiastic to get the most of your experience in the platform.
I advise using Mozilla Firefox for Losena, so you can multi-task multiple active windows at the same time.
