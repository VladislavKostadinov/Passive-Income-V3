Swagbucks

"Launched in Southern California in 2008, Swagbucks.com is the web's most popular loyalty and consumer rewards program. "

Company's statement

"Members of this free rewards service get free gift cards or cash for the everyday activities they're already completing online like searching the web, playing games, watching videos, or shopping online where you'll make money through cash back rebates. There are a number of different ways to earn Swagbucks rewards or SB points that you can easily redeem for Amazon gift cards, PayPal, and other gift cards and great prizes. You'll earn rewards in points, called SB, and you can redeem your points for gift cards or cash. Its 100 SB points for $1 USD in rewards. "

My point of view

"Swagbucks is a very popular platform, in which you can earn cash or gift cards by doing simple tasks, which don't require much skill. In regards to the other side-hustles, I've reviewed, this one has a bigger pay rate in my point of view. You can do surveys (but with surveys it is a bit tricky - you need to qualify for their interest groups), you can play mobile games (with pretty nice rewards), you can complete offers (like sign-ups, etc). You can also install their SwagButton, a browser extension, which provides notifications, when you are on a website with Sales, Coupons and Cash Back Offers.
In my point of view, it is a very nice platform, in which you can earn some passive income by having fun. "

Tips

  • Check "Answer" section for surveys. They pay nice.
  • Check for game offers (both mobile and desktop)*
  • Check the "Shopping" section for Cash Back offers**
  • Invite friends and aqcuire referrals

*You should carefully check the game offers if you like playing games. Some of them pay very little, but others pay very well. You should check the requierements very carefully, because some of them require investment. Also check the operating system of the mobile game offer (if it is for iOS or Android), because you won't get payed if you don't follow the requerements of the offer.

**I have not tried the Shopping section of SwagBucks, but I believe that if provides nice deals if you shop certain items (crypto, food, etc) from a specific place, and you receive a Cash Back from SwagBucks. If you've installed the SwagButton, you can easily check for new offers, without going to the website first.

My earnings

My personal experience with SwagBucks is quite positive. I tried a few mobile games and got some SB points (100 points = 1$), but couldn't reach the minimum withdrawal of 10$. It seemed to me too high and required a lot of effort playing small android games. But then I saw a special offer. It was also a mobile game, but payed 800 SB if you reach a certain level for a certain time. The game was actually fun (Monopoly GO) and I managed to collect the rewards I was aiming for, thus reaching the minimum withdrawal. I received my money in PayPal successfully. So, I recommend always checking the special offers. They pay very well.
