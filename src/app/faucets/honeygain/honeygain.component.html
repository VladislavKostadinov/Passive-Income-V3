My point of view

"Honeygain is my first side hustle. When I decided to give it a try, I already knew I wasn't going to earn much. But Honeygain doesn't require any effort, nor does it slow down your devices or spend a lot of electricity. You just need to register and download it on your computer and mobile device (or tablet). After that it starts using a small portion of your unused MB of internet and generates some income. The more devices you connect - the better your income. But every device must use a different IP (or be connected to a different network). You can't use many devices, connected to the same Wi-Fi for istance. Currently I've connected 2 devices and managed to reach the minimum withdrawal limit of 20$. I have successfully received my earnings to Paypal. "

Tips

  • Connect more devices (with different network IPs)
  • Open daily pot of honey (bonus)*
  • Enable JumpTask** (this way you will earn more, but in the form of Jump Tokens)
  • Enable Content Delivery*** (accessible only in certain locations)

*If you manage to share at least 15 MB daily, you unlock the Honey Pot, which can provide you from 0.005$ to 10$. But, know this, my biggest win at the current moment is 0.5$. Usually, it provides between 0.005$ to 0.015$. Still, there is the chance of a big win, and it takes no more than a minute of your time.

**JumpTask is a partner of Honeygain. It is a different platform, in which you can earn more passive income by completing tasks. JumpTask has its native token - JMPT, which is not launched in the official blockchain, but still has a certain value. If you decide to use Honeygain's partnership with JumpTask, you will receive a 10% bonus.

***Content Delivery is yet another way to share your Internet connection. It includes multiple types of bandwidth-intensive content such as images, videos, audio, streaming, heavy websites, IPTV, VOD, social media, or VoIP services. No, your device storage will not be accessed – as usual, the app only uses your network!
