Tips

  • Use the Faucet (every 30 min.)*
  • Click on the PTC (Pay-To-Click)
  • Plan out your daily Shortlinks**
  • Complete your Daily Tasks***
  • Start Auto Faucet****
  • Invite friends / Acquire referrals

*The faucet claim is available every 30 minutes and gives a random amount of Action Points, between 25-70(I am not completely sure). You can get them every 30 minutes, after you complete a short captcha, to prove you are not a bot. There is also a Daily Bonus, which you can get once per day (~60-150 ACP).

**Shortlinks are tedeous and require more time and patience than normal ads, but provide more Action Points. Once you get used to them and find the ones that suit you the best, it is more of a daily routine than a serious tasks, that requires effort.

***The Daily Tasks is a list of tasks, which can be completed every day. Each taks gives a nice bonus and the list is refreshed daily. If all of them are completed, they provide a nice boost to your daily income and that is why I limit my daily activity to completing all the tasks and activating the Auto Faucet. This way I don't waste much time and still get a sufficient return rate.

****The Auto Faucet is the automated conversion from ACP (Action Points) to Crypto currencies, which you can then withdraw to your direct wallet. You can increase the speed of the Auto Faucet up to 4x and also choose it to convert ACP to up to 12 different crypto currencies at the same time. And don't worry, you can then exchange all your crypto to a certain specific one (that you want to withdraw) without any fee.
