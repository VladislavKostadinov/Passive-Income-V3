Faucets

What Are Crypto Faucets? A crypto faucet allows crypto users and investors to earn small cryptocurrency rewards — basically free – for completing small or simple tasks. Investors generally do not need to make a purchase or put up any additional assets in exchange.

My Faucets

There are a lot of faucets in the web. Most of them look similar. You click on Ads & Shortlinks, complete tasks and offers, play games and answer surveys. These are the most common types of things, you need to do, in order do win some rewards in the form of crypto currency. Most of the time these rewards are quite small, but if you start from 0, they can help you fill your crypto storage just enough, so you can invest in other projects (like cloud-mining or HYIP). Most of these faucets don't hold any risk, but some might make you browse strange links and a lot of adds.
The faucet I currently is FireFaucet.

Other sources of passive income

There are many more sources of passive income in the web. One of the first I ever tried is Honeygain. I also played some mobile games, which reward you, after reaching a certain "checkpoint" or "goal". There are also apps that run in the background and generate you some passive income, like Salad, Kryptex and Crypto Tab. You can also install apps such as Money SMS on your mobile device and get some rewards by receiving SMS. Here you have to choose just a few, otherwise it becomes burdensome on your devices. I will talk about the ones, that I currently use, even though I've tested a lot more.
