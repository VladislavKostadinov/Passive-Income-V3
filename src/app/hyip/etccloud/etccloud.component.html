Company's statement

"Currently, ETC has reached a mutual agreement with Binance, launching TRX staking conversion. ETC stands as the world's first platform managing Binance's energy conversion, uniting global energy users on the ETC cloud computing management platform, achieving a three-party win-win cloud computing management ecosystem. The ETC cloud computing management platform utilizes Binance's TRX staking conversion for energy sales. With a global audience of energy buyers, users charge their ETC accounts by purchasing USDT on Binance and converting it to TRX on the ETC platform. ETC gains energy from Binance through intelligent conversion and sells it to energy consumers. As ETC users participate through staking, they have flexible fund management. ETC acts as a custodian without retaining funds, alleviating global users' concerns about fund security. Users can confidently participate in intelligent conversion through the ETC cloud computing platform."

My point of view

"EtcCloudLtd is an interesting Spanish platform, which claims to have established some kind of third-part cloud computing management system. Their field of action seems to be very serious and I am still trying to grasp the logic. That is because I decided to give the platform a try and managed to withdraw, that is why I want to get to know their actions, so I have a better perspective on their activity. The things I can claim I've tested and understand (partially) are that you invest funds (min. 12 USDT) and try use the Pledge option 6 times / day. Each time, you receive 0.45% ROI. That's it. But in deeper logic, ETC Cloud LTD wants us to believe that by pressing the button, you actually participate in their activity with Binance and third party companies. They claim you, as an investor buy USDT on Binance, by Pledging their funds up to 6 times / day, thus using the the companie's system to automatically convert USDT to TRX on Binance by an intelligent algorithm (or something) and receiving some kind of energy, which it sells to 3rd party users. I will investigate this energy resource further soon."

Tips

  • Register, explore (choose Language) and make an investment*
  • Use the Pledge button in the Trading menu**
  • Increase profit / Withdraw***
  • Invite friends and acquire referrals

All of the icons, I've used for the Tips section are EtcCloudLtd's original images. I did not create or own them.

*To register in the company's platform, you will need to choose your country's area code and enter a valid mobile number (you will need it to receive messages later). You choose your password and enter the website. You can swap between 7 different languages. The platform looks a bit odd on a desktop device. It is very suitable for a mobile (it's one of those platforms). There is even an option to download the mobile version if you feel like it. In the Recharge menu, you can add funds to your account. Make sure to Bind your USDT account (the company prefers a Binance address), where you will receive crypto, when you withdraw. In Account Security, you can change your password and add a transaction password (make sure to remember it).

**In the Trading menu you can actually earn by Pledgingyour invested funds. The proccess is fairly swift. Each Pledge takes ~5 mins, and you can Pledge 6 times per day. But beware, you must to complete all 6 times to receive your daily income after the last one.

***With each passing day your funds increase, which means that your Pledge earning rate increases. The minimum withdraw is 10 USDT and make sure to have at least 12 USDT remaining in your balance at any time, otherwise the Pledge won't work. It's up to you decide when to withdraw or when to benefit from higher daily income.

Good luck!
