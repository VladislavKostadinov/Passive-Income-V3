It has been around from 2021 and has survived until now. Also it has good reviews and its rating is relatevely high. Maybe because of these reasons I decided to give it a try. *Upon registration in BitStarsInv, you are welcomed in the Dashboard, where you can see all of your current investments and earnings. You can browse in the menu and get to know the platform, but it is very user-friendly and you will find your way around. In the Deposit section, you can find different Plans you can choose from. The higher the investment - the higher the ROI. Like everywhere else. I personally chose the first plan, to check how the platform works.

**Every Plan has a different life-cycle, and at its end, you receive your invested capital back as well! But the initial investment has a higher fee than usual, and is processed a bit slower than most places. But as long as the platform is stable and does actual financial transaction, I am willing to accept these minor inconveniences. Plan 1 has the shortest lifecycle of 15 days and a minimum deposit of 10$.

***You can withdraw at any time, and eventhough I am not sure of the exact minimum withdraw, I know it is pretty low. Be sure to add the address in your settings, before initiating a withdraw. You can also choose to reinvest at the end of the investment period or earlier and increase your daily income.

****The Bounty system of BitStarsInv offers a variety of bonuses if you help spread the popularity of the project. Some of them are pretty nice and you should check them out. But, as an advice, you might need to have some activity in the system, before completing a bounty.

Good luck!
