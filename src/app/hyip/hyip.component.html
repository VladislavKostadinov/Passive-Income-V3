Online Investments

The type of online investments I am going to present here hold some signigicant risk, and bare some charecteristics of HYIP (High Yield Investment Programs). Quite frankly, I was kind of reluctant investing in any of them, but with time, I actually decided to invest in a few. They are many different types of investment platforms out there. Sometimes you can find yourself investing in gold, oil, real estate, forex trading or even medicinal cannabis. There a A LOT of scams, so be weary. Such projects try their best to assure you they are legit, while beeing a total fraud. The ones, I usually invest in are the forex traders. And I also tend to play safe and restrain my greed, when I see big ROIs such as > 5%.

Warning

Cloud mining projects hold some risk (most of the time - significant), there are a lot of frauds and scams and many "companies" use the idea of Cloud mining to their advantage, offering fake mining power to the investors and divorcing (or leaving abruptly) quite often, contributing to a loss of capital for the users. Still, the idea is very attractive to the adventuruous and bold web dwellers, who are willing to take a risk with a promise of a generous ROI. But I've discovered that a big number of these cloud mining platforms are a scam. Always do your research, and only invest an amount you are willing to lose!
