Online Investments

The type of online investments I am going to present here hold some signigicant risk, and bare some charecteristics of HYIP (High Yield Investment Programs). Quite frankly, I was kind of reluctant investing in any of them, but with time, I actually decided to invest in a few. They are many different types of investment platforms out there. Sometimes you can find yourself investing in gold, oil, real estate, forex trading or even medicinal cannabis. There a A LOT of scams, so be weary. Such projects try their best to assure you they are legit, while beeing a total fraud. The ones, I usually invest in are mainly traders. And I also tend to play safe and restrain my greed, when I see big ROIs such as > 5%.

Warning

Investing online holds great risk. A huge number of projects are scam or fraud. I have been scammed a few times and lost around 300$. But thanks to that experience, I've started investing strategically and only after I make a thorough research of the platform, I have interest in. Also, when you've seen a lot of scam projects, you start recognizing them early on. One of the major rules in this area of online business is "Invest only what you can afford to lose. I totally agree - you must definitely not give in to your greed, because even if there is a chance to win big, there is an even bigger chance for you to get scammed. I recommend slow and steady investment in strategic moments of the platforms life-cycle.
