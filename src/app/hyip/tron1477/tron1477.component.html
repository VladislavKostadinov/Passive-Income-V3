My point of view

"When I found this new platform, I was a bit sceptical. After I registered, due to curiosity, I found out that is very well made and that it (currently) pays everyday. To test the latter, I had to deposit a small amount of Tron (TRX), because the platform operates with this crypto currency. It has a nice ROI ~7% and two types of investment - Basic (Wallet Account) and Promotional (Promotional Account). My overall opinion of the project is good, I think it might actually be a decent project, but it still holds significant risk. I would plan my investments carefully, proceed with caution and most definitely not invest recklessly. The project is new and not that famous and popular, but it is quite user-friendly and responsive. It is a nice Side Hustle with potential to grow into something more.

The project, though, is not bad, and hold some characteristics of something serious. After you make an investment (the process is quick), you can try the daily withdrawal

**Immediately after investing, you can try the withdraw and test if the app works. And after that you can withdraw daily, while the amount depends on how much you've invested. That is the "Main Wallet" functionality, while the Promotional Wallet gives the opportunity to stake the amount of your choice on different plans (each with different ROI and lifecycle). I've currently tested only the basic functionality, but I learned, that you can transfer funds from promotional to main balance. That might be useful, if you want to increase the TRX in yout Basic Wallet, but don't want to make a new deposit. In this case, if you have TRX in the Promotional Wallet, you can stake it and after the cycle, send the earnings to your main wallet.
