Tips

  • Register, explore and make an investment*
  • Wait for the investment life-cycle ot end**
  • Withdraw / Reinvest***
  • Invite friends and acquire referrals

*After registering in Merobit, you will find yourself in the Dashboard, where you can view your Account Summary, including Balance, Active Deposits and Total Earned. The interface is very user-friendly and you will find the Deposits section easy. After making a deposit, it automatically becomes active and will generate you income everyday at the same time, the deposit was made. Please, be careful and invest on your own risk. I simply can't guarantee how long this project will last. It seems all right at the current moment, but with these projects, you may never know. If you want to invest, maybe make a strategy and invest only a certain amount and make sure to make your own reasearch, before doing so.

**There are two plans, you can choose from - Starter (5% / 30 days) and Premium (6% / 40 days). The minimum investment is 25$ for the Starter and 2500$ for the Premium. After the lifecycle has finished, you get your investment back and you can cancel your plan anytime and get deposit back. You can withdraw anytime, during the lifecycle, not explicitly at the end.

***Withdrawals are not instant, but I've tested them and managed to withdraw 3 times, returning my initial investment. Once again, I implore you, do your own research, before investing. I am just providing you my story and don't want to be held responsible.

Good luck!
