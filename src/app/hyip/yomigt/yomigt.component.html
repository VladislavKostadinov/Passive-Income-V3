My point of view

"Yomigt is an interesting project, which excels in the affiliate system of its users. Depending on the number of affiliates you invite, you get better earning potential - or you get to the next VIP level. You need to be at least VIP 1 to start earning daily, but you can reach that level by investing 30$, even without inviting referrals. But this level will keep you earning for 45 days, before going inactive, unless you invite someone to join, who then invest at least 50$. This will prolonge your VIP 1 plan with 15 more days. If you invite 2 new investors total, you will get to the next VIP level. I haven't invited anyone yet and am still testing the application, but even if you do not invite anyone new, for 45 days you will be able to double your investment. It seems to me like a good chance to earn some extra cash. But, as with every such project, there is significant risk.
The platform has changed. Users can not withdraw, unless they invite someone, who has invested at least 100$. It seems like a pyramid to me now and I do not recommend it. Not unless you have a team, but this still doesn't guarantee you won't get scammed. Please be careful. I will leave the article here, but place warnings.

Tips

  • Register, explore and make an investment*
  • Collect your earnings every day**
  • Use Lucky Draw, Buy Contracts, etc***
  • Invite friends and acquire referrals

Some of the icons, I've used for the Tips section are Yomigt's original images. I did not create or own them.

*The platform bares resemblance to a hybrid between a mobile and a browser app. Upon registering, you will receive 3$ or 6$ bonus, but you will need reach VIP 1 to start earning. In order to do so, you would need to deposit and increase your wallet to at least 30$. This is the minimum deposit (but you can use the registration bonus to decrease the amount you invest to 24-27$).

**Immediately after investing, you can go to Asset menu and start earning. You will be able to click on a button 5 times / day and get from 0.34% ~ 0.39% per click (total of 1.7% - 1.95% daily). So the more you invest, the more you will earn. You will be able to do this task 45 days in a row, but if you invite friends, the duration will increase (+15 days for the first referral, who invests more than 50$). After the 45 days threshold, you can withdraw all your funds (including your initial investment).

***You can also buy contracts, confirm daily checks and spin the lucky draw to earn even more. But these options require bigger investment or contacting of the live support.

Good luck!
