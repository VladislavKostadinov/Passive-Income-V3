StormGain

"All-in-one crypto app with a Bitcoin cloud miner"

Company's statement

"StormGain is a crypto trading platform for everyone. It is a convenient solution for those who want to profit from either the growth or decline of the cryptocurrency market and from long-term investments in crypto assets. Available on any device, StormGain allows you to start trading the most popular and most capitalised coins with a multiplier of up to 500x, or you can just buy and hold crypto.
Inspired by the desire to make cryptocurrency trading more profitable for everyone, StormGain takes the best of investment and cryptocurrency markets and offers a platform for trading via cryptocurrency futures contracts. The platform gives you opportunities in both rising and falling markets and allows you to trade the most popular cryptocurrency pairs with up to 500x multiplier."

My point of view

"StormGain is a platform, that is popular online and has good rating. It combines both crypto trading, staking and cloud mining in one place. The trading is made easier with signals from the platform, when a rise or fall of a currency is eminent. You can also exchange different currencies on their platform to make trading and managing of your finances easier. I haven't tested most of the features, but I have explored a little and mean to make use of its many functionalities, because I find it quite user-friendly and intriguing. The desktop and mobile apps make the platform quite easy to use in any situation. It also provides a 3$ bonus for new users, which is nice. I will talk about ways to use them below."

Tips

  • Register and start using the Cloud Miner*
  • Start trading with the earnings**
  • Withdraw / Reinvest / Stake***
  • Invite friends and acquire referrals

*Upon registration in StormGain, you get a 3$ bonus and you can start using the Cloud Miner. The Cloud Miner has different levels, but for the free user it is on the basic level. It needs to be activated every 4 hours and supplies you with USDT, but you can collect them only when you reach 10$. The first time it is more easy, because there are checkpoints and bonuses along the way.

**After you collect your first 10$ from the Cloud Miner, you can't withdraw them, but you can trade them and withdraw whatever you win from the trades. After some time the bonus funds from the miner disappear and you are left with only the winnings from your trades, so you would need to either trade them, or collect more USDT from the miner.

***You can also invest crypto in the platform and use it to trade, but if you are not adept enough, you might need to go through Stormgain's Training Courses. I plan on doing that soon, because I believe it is not a bad platform to gain some knowedge and experience in crypto trading. The platform does provide video training. As well as Staking, where you get a 2% annual interest. This might be quite useful for accounts with big amounts of crypto, stored in them.

Good luck!

My earnings

When I collected my first 10$ from the Cloud Miner, I payed very little attention how to properly use them and just clicked on the first trading option possible. Needless to say, I lost half of them and the others disappeared after a while (something to do with the way the platforms works). It took me some time to get another 10$ from the Cloud Miner, and this time, I watched the Crypto Tendencies and decided to risk them again. On the next day, I had won 6$. The platform has potential in my point of view, but I do recommend going through its training courses if you are a novice like myself.
