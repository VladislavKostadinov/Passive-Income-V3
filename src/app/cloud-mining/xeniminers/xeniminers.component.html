My point of view

"Xeniminers launched a few days ago, I believe on the 7th of January 2024. It is a very ambitious platform, build as a Blockchain simulation. If you are fond of Blockchains, such as I, you might find this project interesting. The concept is somewhat difficult to grasp at first, but the creators have made their own Blockchain simulation, and you can participate as a Node by buying certain NFTs. This Non-Fundable-Tokens provide you with a virtual miner, which immediately starts functioning on the blockchain. There are three different statuses of these miners and they also possess different rarities. I guess it depends on your luck what kind of miner you will receive by buying an NFT. I will explain about the different statuses below. For now, I will share with you that I find the platform quite interesting and with potential. Your hired miners work tirelessly day and night to support the system, thus generating 'blocks'. After a block is generated, certain miners are chosen to mine it, thus receiving a reward at the end.

Tips

  • Launch App and choose a Blockchain*
  • Go to Marketplace and buy XNC**
  • Register***
  • Mint NFT(s) / Start mining****
  • Withdraw*****
  • Global Data Network / Mining
  • Invite friends and acquire referrals

Some of the icons, I've used for the Tips section are Xeniminers's original images. I did not create or own them.

*You should first launch the app. You will be welcomed to a friendly and well-designed screen with different Blockchains you can choose from. You can view this as options for the different financial assets you wish to trade with. Currently, only the Testnet and the BNB Mainnet are available. If you want to start earning, you would need to select the BNB Mainnet and connect your MetaMask to the platform. If you're new to electronic crypto wallets, you can check my homepage for different solutions, under the Wallets section. After you've reached the Mainnet of Xeniminers, you will find yourself on the Global Data Network page, where you can see how many Nodes are connected, supporting, working or retired, as well as other data you might find interesting, such as the Global power and events, happening in real-time on the platform. It's pretty interesting, really. If a block is found, a certain miner is assigned to it, and this miner might even be yours. Mining it provides rewards in the form of the native token XNC.

**Registration is easy, after you have at least 35 XNC. But please provide my username vladtaltos, when you go through the process, so I receive bonuses and you join my army. The registration process takes 35 XNC (0.0055 BNB).

***The next step is to Mint your first NFT. Doing so provides with with a random miner, which immediately stars supporting the network with a class of Hercules. What does that mean? See below.

****At first the miners are placed in this category (Hercules), which means that they are supporting the network and the creation of new blocks. At some point your miner might be automatically assigned to mine a newly discovered block, this receiving the rank of Worker. The final rank is that of a Retired machine, leaving room for new ones to take its place. You can check how many Hercules, Worker and Retired machines there are currently on the network in the Global Data Network page of the app. There you can also see information about new blocks and assigned machines, as well as other global information.

*****Withdraw is made at the Bank of Xeniminers. There you can claim XNC as BNB directly to your MetaMask or you can also clain RPS, which I suppose is some kind of bonuses (referral and other), that you receive throughout your journey in the network. The price of XNC is the same as the buying one - 35 XNC = 0.0055 BNB.

For additional information, you can check the official Whitepaper. If you have difficulties reading Spanish, you can translate it with the browser's build-in tools.on. Good luck!
