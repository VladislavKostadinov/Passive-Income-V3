Tips

  • Connect your wallet (pref. MetaMask)*
  • Invest / Hire miners**
  • Withdrawal / Compound every 24h***
  • Join Telegram group / Get bonuses ****
  • Invite friends and acquire referrals

*The registration process is simple and straightforward. To participate, you need to connect your wallet (preferably MetaMask - has lower taxes) and you are prompted to do so on loading the website in your browser. It is not difficult, you just need to have an account and need to be on BSC network. If you don't have a wallet, you can check my homepage for available options. For this platform, it is recommended you choose MetaMask.

**The investment process is also easy and straight-forward. You just need to plan the amount of BNB you want to invest (30 BNB max, 2$ in BNB min). You are prompted to accept the transaction in MetaMask (you still need to have some BNB in the wallet to perform this action) and after accepting the transaction, you hire miners in the website. I've tested it and it works. If MetaMask provides an error, you can try to change the Gas Fee by click on Site Suggested and choose one of the other standart options - for example Market. They can be found in MetaMask during any transaction, just search for Site Suggested and click it.

***After investing, the timer starts counting-down from 24h and the next day you will be provided with two options - Withdraw and Compound. There are some specifics here.
Withdraw - you can use this option and will instantly receive the amount in your wallet (after confirming the transcation), but if you try to withdraw the next day, you might see a 90% tax accompanying this option. This is made to secure the longetivity of the miners and is part of the strategic design of the platform itself. So don't let that bother you. Instead, you should "Compound". Compound - or 'reinvest'. This option will trigger a new transaction in MetaMask (you still have to pay the gas fee), and leads to reinvesting your daily earnings to your total deposit capital. In example - you've invested 10$ in BNB crypto currency. The daily yield is 3%. You receive ~0.3$ daily. If you compound 3 days, your total investment will become 10$+3x0.3$ = ~11$. Why is this important? Your miners have limited "life". They will work until they've reached 200% of their initial value. If you increase that initial value strategically, your miners will live longer. Don't abuse it, though. Compounding has also provides a cummulative 1% bonus every day (up to 10%), which is refreshed after a successful withdraw.

****Joining the official Telegram channel will help you understand more about the project, be eligible to big bonuses, participate in discussions, meet the admins and more. Also, you can ask any questions there. The project is Spanish, but don't let that stop you. Telegram has a build-in translator.
Some of the other bonuses in the platform is the Lottery and Highest Investor Competition, which can provide nice bonuses, when you invest. For every 0.01 BNB you invest or compound, you receive a Lottery ticket (max 50).

Good luck!
