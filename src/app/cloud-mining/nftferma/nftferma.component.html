Tips

  • Make a good investment strategy*
  • Upgrade your financial assets**
  • Get bonus rewards, win competitions, complete quests***
  • Invite friends and acquire referrals

Some of the icons, I've used for the Tips section are NftFerma's original images. I did not create or own them.

*I strongly recommend a good investment strategy. I've been investing for 6 months and the project has been paying daily. But recently, there have been changes and the platform has shown more econimic-based characterisics, which require more strategic thinking. Currently the project does not allow more than 200% ROI. When you double your earnings, your account gets restarted and you start from beginning. You need to buy Oil Wells, upgrade them and so on. Supposedly, when you reach 100% ROI, your wells dry-up and you will need to find another source of income. There is a new section of the game, called "Deposits" and it provides the means to complete your strategic investment and double your funds. The strategy I recommend is checking the "Well Exchange" , which is an auction for Oil Wells, find offers from other players with a discount and buy some assets. You should plan ahead and know how much you want to invest. After you reach your limit, make sure all your Oil Wells are at least Level 2. Because when they dry-up, you won't be able sell them in the "Well Exchange". When the dry-up occurs, you just need to sell your Oil Rigs and use the received TRX (Tron) to invest in the "Deposit" section. You can not directly withdraw that amount, because it is going to be in your 'For shopping' wallet, but you can invest them and receive daily income between 0.1% - 1.5%.

**Upgrading your Oil Wells is quite important. You get better ROI (in %) and you can also sell them, later on, when they dry-up. Their new owner will receive them full, not empty, so they won't lose anything. But you can not sell a Level 1 Oil Well. It needs to be at least Level 2.

***The project is full of bonuses. Such as Daily bonus, Withdrawal bonus, Investment bonus and many more. There are also Contests (Investment, Referral), Promo Codes in the Telegram chat (on the holidays), as well as Quests (some of which provide a lot of TRX). They all depend on your activity in the platform, and increase proportionally with it.
